KHARKIV NATIONAL UNIVERSITY OF RADIOELECTRONICS


TUTION FEE: 2500$(USD) Per year

Kharkiv is not only the first capital of Ukraine, but also the student capital. Among more than 20 universitiesofour city Kharkiv National University of Radio Electronics (NURE) is one of theoldest technology universities in Ukraine, founded in 1930. NURE has strong research and education capabilities in electrical engineering, electronics, telecommunications and computer technologies........NURE pioneered the development of distance education in Ukraine. It has a great expertise in introduction of innovative technologies into education and business. The University aims to integrate into the international, particularly European, scientific and educational space.

National aerospace University (KHARKIV AVIATION INSTITUTE)


TUTION FEE: 3000$(USD) Per year

The National Aerospace University KhAI is a leading Ukrainian technical University that provides a full cycle of higher education in the field of aviation and aerospace engineering. Since the University foundation in 1930, its history is closely connected with aircraft engineering and science global development. ........The National Aerospace University KhAI participates in the International Space program EAlfa and some other scientific programs together with the USA, Germany, Mexico, Japan, Netherlands and China. The 21st century is the era of rapid development of aviation and cosmonautics. Scientists and teachers of the university are looking into the future with optimism and confidence. They are ready to solve any problem time may offer.

Kharkiv University of AIRFORCE


Ivan Kozhedub Kharkiv University of Air Force is a multidisciplinary higher educational institution with a highly qualified faculty, up-to-date educational and material facility, developed infrastructure. The University was founded in 1930......... Kharkiv University of Air Force has the IV level of accreditation and trains students in 7 majors and specialties. The University also trains international students in all the accredited specialties at the level of all the qualified requirements of bachelor, specialist and master on the basis of the international contracts of Ukraine, state programs, contracts drawn by the University with legal entities and individuals.

Kyiv aviation University


Kyiv Aviation Institute was founded in 1933. It is one of the most respectable, oldest engineering universities of Ukraine recognized all over the world.For almost 80 years of its history more than 200,000 highly skilled professionals has been trained in this higher aviation educational institution.........Among them there are well-known scientists, heads of aviation companies, enterprises, organizations and institutions that provide aircraft flights, their maintenance and repair, transportation of passengers and cargo.

National Technical University Kharkiv POLYTECHNIC INSTITUTE


The National Technical University Kharkiv Polytechnic Institute NTU KhP, which was founded in 1885 is one of the oldest technical higher education institution of the Left-Bank Ukraine. During its more than 130-year history the University has become an integral part of the scientific, technical, intellectual and cultural history of Ukraine.........The name of a Noble Prize winner L.D. Landau and the names of worldwide known scientists such as academicians N.N. Beketov, P.P. Budnikov, A.K Valter, the honorary doctors D.I. Mendeleev and N.Y. Zhukovskyi as well as the name of professor V.L Kyrpychov, the founder of NTU KhPI and Kyiv Polytechnic, and also many other famous names are connected with the National Technical University Kharkiv Polytechnic Institute.
